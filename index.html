What do you think that could be the new beauty trend or new beauty channel arising in Vietnam in the next 2 years

Based on the current state of the Vietnamese beauty industry, there are a few potential trends or channels that could emerge in the next two years:

Skinimalism

Skinimalism, a minimalist approach to skincare that emphasizes simplicity and natural beauty, is a beauty trend that has been gaining popularity around the world in recent years, and it's expected to continue to be a major trend in Vietnam for the next few years due to several factors. Firstly, Vietnamese consumers are becoming more aware of the importance of skincare and are looking for ways to take care of their skin in a simple and effective way. Secondly, the climate in Vietnam is hot and humid, which can make heavy skincare routines uncomfortable and impractical. Skinimalism offers a way to take care of the skin without being weighed down by heavy products. Skinimalism in Vietnam is all about achieving healthy and glowing skin with minimal effort. This means that skincare routines will be simplified, with fewer products and steps. Instead of relying on a long list of products, consumers will focus on using high-quality, multi-tasking products that can address multiple skin concerns at once.

Using skincare tools at home

With advances in technology, consumers are now able to access professional-grade skin care treatments from the comfort of their own homes. Home skin care equipment includes devices such as facial cleansing brushes, LED light therapy devices, microcurrent devices, and more. These devices offer a range of benefits, including deep cleaning, exfoliation, and anti-aging effects. One of the main reasons why home skin care equipment is becoming more popular in Vietnam is its convenience. Many consumers don't have the time or resources to visit a professional spa or salon regularly, so using home skin care equipment allows them to achieve similar results from the comfort of their own homes. Another reason why home skin care equipment is becoming more popular is due to its affordability. While professional-grade skin care treatments can be expensive, many home skin care devices are now available at affordable price points, making them accessible to a wider range of consumers.
